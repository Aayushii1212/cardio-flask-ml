About CardioPredict

Our Mission

CardioPredict is an advanced machine learning system designed to help individuals assess their cardiovascular disease risk. Our mission is to make heart health assessment accessible, accurate, and actionable for everyone.

We believe that early detection and awareness are crucial in preventing cardiovascular diseases, which remain the leading cause of death globally. By leveraging cutting-edge AI technology and comprehensive health data, we aim to empower individuals to take control of their heart health.

What We Do

Our platform uses an XGBoost machine learning model trained on over 68,000 patient records to provide accurate cardiovascular disease risk predictions. The system analyzes multiple health parameters including:

Demographics

Age, gender, height, and weight

Vital Signs

Blood pressure measurements

Lab Results

Cholesterol and glucose levels

Lifestyle

Physical activity, smoking, alcohol

Why Trust Us

Data-Driven Accuracy: Our model achieves 74.4% accuracy on test data, trained on a comprehensive dataset of real patient records with validated outcomes.

Scientific Methodology: We use XGBoost, one of the most powerful and widely-used machine learning algorithms in healthcare, combined with proper data preprocessing and feature engineering.

Transparent Process: We provide detailed explanations of our methodology and are committed to transparency in how predictions are made.

Continuous Improvement: Our system is regularly updated and validated against new data to maintain accuracy and relevance.
